What is Divastra JEE Ambassador Program?

Every IIT JEE aspirant is facing lots of problems and feels helpless during this journey. So Divastra has decided to give personal mentorship, guidance, motivation and hand holding support at each and every path of this IIT JEE Journey. So Divastra has launched DIVASTRA JEE Personal Mentorship Program for this at just Rs.199/month. But many students are not aware about it. So if you feel that you can help students by telling about this mentorship program and save there future then Divastra is ready to pay you for this Social Work. So this is DIvastra JEE Ambassador Program. You will be paid for each student who joins through you. Finally you become an ambassador of Divastra JEE by doing this social work.


How much I will be payed?

  • You will be paid Rs.20 for each student.
  • You will get Rs.50 extra for first 10 students. Rs.60 extra for next 10 students and so on. It means for each 10 students you will be paid extra money. With each 10 students this extra money will keep increasing.
  • If you bring maximum number of students among all Divastra JEE ambassadors in a particular month then you will get double payment.
  • Payment will be made at the end of each month.
  • If you bring atleast 50 students then you will get special mention on this website.
  • For Example:
    If you bring 30 students in a month then you will get 30x20=600. Extra money= 50+60+70=180 If you would have brought most number of students then you will get double payment. So Total Earning for 30 students: 2x(600+180)=Rs.1560

OMG! This is much more than pocket money.


What will I have to do to become Divastra JEE Ambassador and earn money?

First fill this form to register as Divastra JEE ambassador: Ambassador form
you will get a telegram channel and group link after submitting the form. Join Telegram channel to recieve important updates and relevant resources which will help you to convince students. Join telegram group to ask your queries.

After Registration just start sharing about this mentorship program with students. Ask them to fill this google form worth Rs.199/month: Mentorship form
There is a section of Referral Gmail ID in the form. You have to ask students to enter your Gmail ID there. Through this only I will be able to identify that they have came through you.


What does students get in this Divastra JEE Personal Mentorship Program?

Students will get Personal guidance, mentorship, motivation, hand holding supoort at each and every path of their IIT JEE Journey. Some study material, topper's secret tips tricks, time management skills will also be given int he session. These sessions are held on live Google meet every Saturday, Sunday from 6PM to 8PM. Link of these sessions are sent in a telegram group and channel. Students will get these telegram group and channel links after filling the mentorship form: Mentorship Form
