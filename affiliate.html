Register

Important Instruction

While promoting any Divastra program/course to students share your G-Mail ID with students with which you will fill the Divastra Affiliate registration google form. Registration google form of any Divastra program/course have a section of referral G-Mail ID. Whenever a student enters your G-Mail ID (through which you will fill the Divastra Affiliate registration google form) in the referral G-Mail ID section of their any Divastra program/course registration google form then we come to know that the student have registered through you and your affiliate commission for that registration gets credited into your account.

Your affiliate commission is not limited for new students only. If the same student registers again for any Divastra program/course by filling your G-Mail ID in registration google form then again you will get affiliate commission of that registration. So as many times a student will keep registering for any Divastra program/course by filling your G-Mail ID in registration google form that many time you will get affiliate commission.

You will get 50% affiliate commission on each registration. Divastra currently have 2 program/course:

1. Divastra Mentorship Program (₹200/- per month)
